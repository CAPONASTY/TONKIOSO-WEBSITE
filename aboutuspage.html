Our Story

Tonkioso Fashion emerged in 2024 as the brainchild of Lourdes Genevieve, a young and visionary designer born in 2002. From an early age, Lourdes displayed a natural affinity for the world of fashion, but it wasn’t until her teenage years that her distinctive style began to take shape. Influenced heavily by her deep love for rock music, Lourdes found inspiration in the bold, rebellious, and often eccentric fashion statements made by legendary rock icons. She admired their unapologetic individuality and the way they used clothing to communicate their unique identities and break societal norms.

The name "Tonkioso" embodies a sense of rebellion and power, a brand that challenges the ordinary while embracing a timeless elegance. Lourdes envisioned Tonkioso as a brand that would bridge the gap between edgy, alternative fashion and high-end sophistication.

One of the most pivotal influences in Lourdes’ journey to launching her own brand was her godfather, Richard Owino. A legend in the fashion world, Richard founded the globally renowned Rick Owino and Co fashion brand, known for its innovative designs and attention to detail. From a young age, Lourdes was exposed to the inner workings of the fashion industry, learning the trade from her godfather’s side. She often accompanied Richard to runway shows, design meetings, and fabric sourcing trips. Watching him work instilled in her an appreciation for high-quality craftsmanship and a passion for creating timeless pieces.

Richard’s mentorship played a crucial role in shaping Lourdes’ approach to fashion. He taught her the importance of designing clothes that not only looked beautiful but also told a story. He encouraged her to experiment with different fabrics, textures, and silhouettes, which helped her refine her aesthetic.

By 2024, after years of immersion in the world of fashion, Lourdes was ready to launch Tonkioso. The brand reflected her unique identity, blending the gritty, rebellious spirit of rock music with the refined elegance she had learned under Richard’s guidance. Tonkioso quickly gained attention for its bold, structured designs, often incorporating elements like leather, metal accents, and sharp tailoring, balanced by soft, luxurious fabrics and intricate detailing.

Each Tonkioso collection carries the spirit of the rock stars Lourdes admired, fused with the meticulous craftsmanship she inherited from her godfather. Lourdes envisions Tonkioso as a brand that empowers individuals to express their true selves through fashion—mixing classic silhouettes with daring, unconventional elements. By pushing boundaries and staying true to her roots, Lourdes aims to make Tonkioso a timeless yet forward-thinking brand in the global fashion scene.
